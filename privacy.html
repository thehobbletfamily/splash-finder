Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Who We Are

Splashfinders (splashfinders.com) is a free community resource helping families in Will, Kendall, and Grundy Counties, Illinois find local splash pads. We are operated by a local mom and are not affiliated with any park district or municipality.

Information We Collect

Splashfinders does not collect personal information from visitors. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal data to use the app.

If you use the "Submit a Splash Pad" form, that form is operated by Google Forms and subject to Google's privacy policy. We receive only the information you voluntarily submit through that form.

Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

Splashfinders participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click a product link on our "What to Pack" page and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely believe are useful for families visiting splash pads.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site (such as which parks are most viewed). This data is anonymous and aggregated — it cannot be used to identify individual visitors.

Amazon and Google may use cookies when you interact with their services through links on our site. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Third-Party Links

Splashfinders contains links to Google Maps for directions and to Amazon.com for product recommendations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

Children's Privacy

Splashfinders does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The site is intended for use by parents and caregivers.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at hello@splashfinders.com.
